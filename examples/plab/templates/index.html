Welcome to the Automated Assessment System for the PLab unit - year 2017/18.

This is an example of the feedback that the students for the PLab unit received continuously during the semester, based on their commits to their dedicated SVN repository.
This example version includes only a few toy-example submissions. The uncensored version of this example requires each group of students to authenticate to see their personal feedback, and consisted of around 100 groups of 3 students each.

Last update: $lastupdate$.

All information in this site aims at providing feedback on the group assignment, and does not directly reflects the grade that each group will get at each evaluation phase.
